## Material

Molten crystal core.

## Weight

Heavy

## Glow

Warm orange pulse.

## Idle Animation

Slow heartbeat.

## Match Animation

## Destroy Animation

Crystal dust + ember particles.

## Sound

Low warm thump.

## Lore

Born inside dying stars.
